French Press Method

French Press Essentials

A classic immersion brewing method that produces a heavy-bodied, flavorful cup.

  • 1

    Coarsely grind 30g of coffee beans.

  • 2

    Add coffee to the press and pour 500ml of hot water (93°C).

  • 3

    Stir gently and let it steep for 4 minutes.

  • 4

    Slowly press the plunger down and serve immediately.
